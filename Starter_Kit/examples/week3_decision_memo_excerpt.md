# Week 3 Example - Decision Memo Excerpt

Recommendation: run a limited pilot for the top 30 percent of customers ranked by decision-time risk.

Excluded features:

- `retained_next_month`: target outcome.
- `post_offer_response`: not known before the offer decision.

Business logic:

- Offer cost: 25.
- Expected saved margin: 120.
- Break-even save rate: 25 / 120 = 20.8 percent.

Limit:

This policy prioritises customers for a pilot. It does not prove the offer causes retention.
